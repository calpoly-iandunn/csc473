## Super Sampling

$$ U_s = -0.5 + \frac{i + 0.5 + \left(  -0.5 + \frac{m + 0.5}{s}  \right) }{w} $$

$$ V_s = -0.5 + \frac{j + 0.5 + \left(  -0.5 + \frac{n + 0.5}{s}  \right) }{h} $$

Where $$ m, n $$ is the sub-pixel, and $$ s $$ is the number of sub-pixel super-samples.
